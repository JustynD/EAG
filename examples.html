Countries with Exemplar Sustainability Records

Iceland

Iceland is the most environmentally sustainable country according to the Environmental Performance Index (EPI). It ranks highest due to a low level of air pollution and high quality of water. They also maintain a climate change policy as enforced by the national government that helps with their environmental initiatives.

Switzerland

Switzerland ranks second on the EPI due to its resourcefulness. Switzerland lacks many resources but is rich in wood; rather than import resources they do not have naturally, they make use of the wood. Switzerland also maintains an government agency dedicated to sustainability that ensures the environment remains on the national agenda.

Costa Rica

Costa Rica is rich in rainforests. Much like Sweden, Costa Rica maintains a government agency dedicated to sustainability. The government is heavily invested in the issue of conservation. Costa Rica is also home to more than 10 thousand plant species.

Sweden

Sweden is ranked high on the EPI due to its strong performance in environmental health and water management. The Swedish government has committed to having zero net greenhouse gas emissions by 2050





Norway

Norway has a very extensive water and forest conservation program which leads it to be the 5th ranked sustainable country. These efforts not only help the environment, but the citizens as well.
